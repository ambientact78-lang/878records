Bio

Ambient Act is the product of reinvention—a Syrian-American artist who began as a songwriter weaving between rap, neo-soul, and chill pop, and eventually stepped into production so he could create a world entirely his own. Singing, rapping, producing—he treats them all as different tools to express the same emotion.

Ambient Act is the product of reinvention—a Syrian-American artist who began as a songwriter weaving between rap, neo-soul, and chill pop, and eventually stepped into production so he could create a world entirely his own. Singing, rapping, producing—he treats them all as different tools to express the same emotion.

From opening for Mexican OT in Texas to building 878 Records in Connecticut, he continues to chase new sounds and new textures. His music is atmospheric but grounded, introspective but always evolving—an ongoing experiment in feeling.
